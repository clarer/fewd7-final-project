How we can help you

There is a common misconception in the small business world that building a powerful, intuitive and well designed website is a costly and time consuming experience. Native Technology is here to show you this is not the case! Let us take the headache out of your online vision and help you create a chic, functional, cost effective website that adds value to your business.

Initial Consultation

Using our industry expertise, we begin with a free initial consultation where we discuss your business needs and make a recommendation on what solution is best suited for you.

Website Design & Build

Once you're happy with the solution, we start building your cost effective, customised website. We'll help you choose a great design as well as work with you on the content.

Hosting & Maintenance

When your website 'goes live', your selected monthly website hosting package commences. All packages include monthly back ups and security updates.
